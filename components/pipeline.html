How It Works

From Brief to Production Data.

Our 8-stage structured pipeline delivers QA-verified, production-ready annotation data — consistently.

1
Day 0
Discovery & Brief

We define scope, annotation schema, quality targets, and data classification requirements with your team.

2
Day 1
Taxonomy Design

Our ontology specialists build a custom annotation taxonomy aligned to your model architecture and output format.

3
Day 1–2
Annotator Selection & Training

We select, certify, and train a specialist annotator squad from our 50,000+ global workforce for your domain.

4
Day 2–3
Pilot Annotation

A statistically significant pilot batch is completed, reviewed, and iterated to lock in quality benchmarks before scale.

5
Day 3+
Production Annotation

Full-scale annotation runs in parallel across your dataset, with continuous throughput monitoring and SLA tracking.

6
Ongoing
Multi-Layer QA

Every annotation passes human expert review, inter-annotator agreement analysis, and automated consistency checks.

7
Delivery
Format & Delivery

Data delivered in your required format — JSON, COCO, YOLO, custom schema — via secure API or direct transfer.

8
Post-Delivery
Iteration & Scale

Dedicated success management, active learning feedback loop, and capacity to scale to millions of annotations/month.
